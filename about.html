✦ How We Began ✦

A Dream Plated
With Purpose

STACKLY was born from a single conviction — that great food should tell a story. Founded in 2018 in the heart of Salem, Tamil Nadu, we set out to rewrite what fine dining could mean in South India: not an imitation of elsewhere, but a confident expression of here.

Our founders, passionate about Tamil culinary heritage, travelled across three continents studying technique, flavour, and hospitality. What they brought back was distilled into STACKLY — a restaurant where traditional Tamil ingredients meet modern culinary philosophy.

Today, STACKLY stands as one of the most celebrated dining destinations in Tamil Nadu, honoured with national awards and loved by thousands of guests who return not just for the food, but for how it makes them feel.

✦ Our Philosophy ✦

What We Stand For

Four principles that guide every dish, every interaction, every moment.

Farm to Table

We source our ingredients directly from trusted local farmers across Tamil Nadu, ensuring peak freshness and supporting sustainable agriculture.

Culinary Craft

Every dish is a product of obsessive attention — from the 72-hour slow braises to the hand-painted dessert plates. Craft is never compromised.

Warm Hospitality

In Tamil culture, a guest is God. That belief runs through every team member — from the first greeting to the final farewell.

Global Vision

We respect our roots while remaining curious about the world — drawing on global technique to elevate the flavours of home.

✦ Milestones ✦

Our Journey

Eight years of growth, recognition, and relentless pursuit of excellence.

2018

Doors Open

STACKLY opens its first restaurant in Salem with a 40-cover dining room, a team of six, and an unwavering vision to bring world-class cuisine to Tamil Nadu.

2019

The Chef's Table Launches

We introduce the Chef's Table experience — an intimate 6-guest kitchen-side tasting menu that sells out within hours of each release and gains national press attention.

2021

Times Food Award

STACKLY is named Best Fine Dining Restaurant in Tamil Nadu by the Times Food Guide, placing us on the national culinary map for the first time.

2023

Private Dining & Events Wing

We expand our premises to include a dedicated private dining suite and a full-service catering operation, bringing the STACKLY experience to events across Tamil Nadu.

2024

Three Michelin Stars

STACKLY becomes the first restaurant in Salem to receive three Michelin Stars — a crowning recognition of our kitchen's consistency, creativity, and craft.

2026

Looking Forward

With over 12,000 guests served and 850 events hosted, we continue to grow — staying rooted in Salem while setting our sights on new culinary horizons.
