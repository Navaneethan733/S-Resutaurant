✦ Getting Here ✦

Find Your Way
to STACKLY

STACKLY · Salem
Open in Google Maps
01

By Car

Take the Salem bypass via NH-44. Exit at Five Roads junction towards Omalur Main Road. We're 400m on your left. Free valet parking available from 6PM.

02

By Auto / Cab

Ask for STACKLY at Five Roads, Omalur Main Road. A 10-minute ride from Salem New Bus Stand and 15 minutes from Salem Junction Railway Station.

03

By Bus

Board any city bus towards Omalur from Salem New Bus Stand. Deboard at Five Roads stop — we're right across the junction.

04

Parking

Complimentary valet parking is available every evening. A dedicated parking bay is located 50m west of the main entrance for daytime visits.
